Two new professors join the Computer Science department faculty this fall. For the students who would like to get to know them better, *The Icarus* will be publishing interviews with each of them this month.

Dr. Sarah Ita Levitan will be teaching the linguistics elective this fall and then CSCI 265 (Computing Theory) this spring.

Dr. Tong will be teaching CSCI 135, the first of the Software Analysis and Design sequence.
